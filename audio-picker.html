Dino Arena — Audio Picker

Audition each candidate and pick a favourite per category. Tell the coordinator which file you want for each slot and a follow-up will wire them in as the final in-game sounds. The DEFAULT tag marks what is currently wired into the game.

All samples are CC0 or royalty-free (Kenney CC0 footsteps; Mixkit Free SFX; OpenGameArt CC0 breathing). See CREDITS.md. T-Rex offers both the research-accurate eerie low rumble (closed-mouth boom) and a classic cinematic roar — A/B them and choose whichever feels best.

Files live in assets/audio/candidates/. Defaults are copies in assets/audio/ referenced by src/config.js → AUDIO.samples.
