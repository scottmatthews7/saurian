Gameplay

No weapons. Just speed, nerve, and the dark.

A procedural 3D valley that escalates as the light fails. Every run is a race against the dusk and the things that hunt in it.

🦖

Outrun the apex

A relentless T-Rex hunts by sight. You're faster at a full sprint — but stamina runs dry, and a jog won't save you.

🦅

Evade the packs

Raptors hunt in coordinated packs — fast, fearless, and built to flank. Break their line of sight or get swarmed.

🌒

Race the dusk

As night falls the predators grow bolder and the valley darkens. The longer you last, the deadlier it gets.

🩸

Punch, kick, dash

Bare-handed strikes, a stamina sprint, and a dash with brief invulnerability to slip a bite at the last instant.

🥚

Scavenge to survive

Glowing eggs dotted across the valley restore health and stamina — grab them on the run to stay one stride ahead.

🦕

A living valley

A fleeing herbivore herd, diving pterosaurs, six dinosaur species, and a tension heartbeat that quickens as the rex closes.
